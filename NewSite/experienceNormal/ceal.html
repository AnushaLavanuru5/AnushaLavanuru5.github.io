Overview

The Community History through AR project leverages Augmented Reality (AR) to preserve and showcase the rich cultural heritage of the Harlem region. By blending modern AR technologies with community storytelling, the project empowers residents to engage with their history in a meaningful and interactive way while fostering civic pride and participation.
