Overview

The PolXR project leverages Extended Reality (XR) technologies to revolutionize geospatial data visualization and collaborative scientific analysis. It combines state-of-the-art multi-user environments with immersive exploration tools to analyze polarimetric radar datasets, enabling interdisciplinary research and better decision-making in glaciology and Earth sciences.
