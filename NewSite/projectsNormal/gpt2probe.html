Probing GPT-2 Layers for Understanding and Relationship Analysis

Overview

The GenAI - Probing GPT-2 Layers for Understanding and Relationship Analysis project explores how GPT-2's layers understand linguistic relationships, such as entailments, contradictions, and neutrality. Using structural probing techniques, this project dissects GPT-2's layers to examine the model's learning and semantic understanding.

Probing involves capturing hidden states from specific layers and evaluating their performance in understanding relationships between sentence pairs.
