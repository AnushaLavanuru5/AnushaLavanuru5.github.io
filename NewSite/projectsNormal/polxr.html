PolXR

Overview

PolXR is an innovative platform developed under the VISER (Visualization for Enhanced Scientific Exploration in Research) initiative. It leverages Extended Reality (XR) technologies to transform polarimetric geospatial data into an immersive 3D environment, enabling researchers to analyze and interpret complex datasets interactively. By combining cutting-edge visualization tools with user-friendly interfaces, PolXR revolutionizes geospatial research for environmental and climate change studies.

Tech Stack

  • Programming Languages: C#
  • Libraries: XR, MRTK
  • Tools:Unity, Blender

Key Features

  • Immersive Data Exploration: Transforms polarimetric geospatial datasets into 3D and XR environments, enhancing understanding and facilitating advanced analysis.
  • Cross-Platform Compatibility: Supports both VR and AR platforms, allowing researchers to engage with data through immersive XR tools.
  • Interactive User Interface: Offers intuitive controls for filtering, scaling, and rotating data visualizations, tailored to the needs of scientific exploration.
  • Seamless Integration: Integrates smoothly with existing polarimetric data workflows, ensuring compatibility with widely used geospatial formats.
